榕树，桑科。常绿大乔木，但若盆栽则根系生长受限，多呈灌木状。如为乔木则茎干粗、多分枝、枝叶浓密，全株无毛，树冠伞形至圆形，深根性。老枝条常有气生根，可为遮荫树、市街道路之行道树、防风、防火树，以及盆栽树。

实用上，榕树多半作为「防风、防火树」，精神上，榕树也做为避邪树。那年冬日，年轻作家过世，祭吊行前同事在路边摘了几片榕叶放在黑色大衣口袋，说是和端午节门板上挂着艾草、榕枝、香茅叶一样，都是避邪、避晦气。那日许多人的口袋都有几片榕叶，年轻作家的鬼魂想必隔着远远地和我们道别。

大榕树下总会有一群老人，不管是都市的公园，或乡镇的土地公庙，老人和大榕树形成共生，相互依存。由于榕树「阳性树，树性极强，不择土壤；根系分歧而广布，耐风，耐潮，对空气污染之抗害力特强。」是这样的因素吧，让榕树动辄形成五十年、一百年老树，茂茂荫荫的枝桠绿叶，成了天然的凉棚茶馆，虽然简陋，却十分自在，让无拘无束退休的老人摆上桌椅，或坐或卧，下棋、喝茶、闲聊聚集。

Banyan tree, Sangke. Evergreen trees, but if potted, root growth is restricted, mostly shrub-like. If it is an arbor, the stem is thick, multi-branched, the branches and leaves are dense, the whole plant is glabrous, the crown is umbrella-shaped to round, deep rooted. Old branches often have aerial roots, which can be shade trees, street trees on city streets, windproof, fireproof trees, and potted trees.

Practically speaking, the banyan tree is mostly used as a "windproof and fireproof tree." In spirit, the banyan tree is also used as a tree for avoiding evil. On the winter day of that year, the young writer passed away. Before the festival, his colleagues picked a few banyan leaves on the roadside and put them in a black coat pocket. Avoid evil and avoid evil spirits. Many people had a few banyan leaves in their pockets that day, and the ghost of the young writer must have said farewell to us from afar.

There will always be a group of old people under the big banyan tree, whether it is an urban park or a public temple in the town, the old man and the big banyan tree form a symbiosis and interdependence. Because the banyan tree is "positive tree, the tree is extremely strong, and it does not choose the soil; the root system is divergent and widely distributed, and it is resistant to wind and moisture, and it is extremely resistant to air pollution." Is it such a factor, let the banyan tree form for fifty years A hundred-year-old tree and lush shades of green foliage have become a natural pergola teahouse. Although simple, it is very comfortable, allowing unrestricted retired elderly people to put on tables and chairs, or sit or lie down, play chess, drink tea, chat Gather.
