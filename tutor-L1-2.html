我的家有五個人，爸爸媽媽，一個哥哥和一個妹妹。我的哥哥是一位工程師，現在二十五歲；我的妹妹是一位護士，比我小兩歲。我哥哥喜歡打電動，妹妹每天看電視。這些事情我都不喜歡，我喜歡出門運動和遛狗。我有兩隻狗，一隻叫米奇，一隻叫米妮，他們是好朋友。

Wǒ de jiā yǒu wǔ gè rén, bàba māma, yī ge gēge hé yī ge mèimei. Wǒ de gēge shì yī wèi gōngchéngshī, xiànzài èr shí wǔ suì; wǒ de mèimei shì yī wèi hùshì, bǐ wǒ xiǎo liǎng suì. wǒ gēge xǐhuān dǎ diàndòng, mèimei měitiān kàn diànshì. Zhè xiē shì qíng wǒ dōu bù xǐhuān, wǒ xǐhuān chū mén yùndòng hé liúgǒu. wǒ yǒu liǎng zhī gǒu, yī zhī jiào mǐqí, yī zhī jiào mǐní, tā men shì hǎo péngyǒu.

There are five people in my family, father and mother, an older brother and a younger sister. My brother is an engineer, and now he is twenty-five years old; my sister is a nurse, and she is two years younger than me. My brother likes to play video games, and my sister watches TV every day. I don't like these things. I like going out and walking dogs. I have two dogs, one called Mickey and another called Minnie, they are good friends.
